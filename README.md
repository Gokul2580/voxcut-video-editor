## What is VoxCut?

VoxCut is an intelligent, browser-based video editing application designed to democratize content creation. We believe that everyone deserves the tools to share their voice with the world — regardless of their financial background or technical expertise.

Our mission is simple: **Remove the barriers. Amplify the creativity.**

---

## The Problem We Solve

The content creation industry has an unspoken gatekeeping problem:

- Professional editing software costs hundreds of dollars per year
- Learning curves are steep and intimidating for beginners
- High-end hardware is required to run most editing tools
- Hours are wasted on repetitive tasks like cutting silences and selecting takes

This creates an unfair advantage for those with resources, while talented creators from modest backgrounds struggle to compete.

**We refuse to accept this reality.**

---

## Our Solution

VoxCut brings professional-grade editing capabilities directly to your browser — completely free and accessible on any device.

### Intelligent Automation
- **Automatic Silence Detection & Removal** — Our AI identifies and removes awkward pauses, dead air, and filler moments instantly
- **Smart Take Selection** — When you record multiple versions, VoxCut helps you identify and select the best performance
- **Video Stabilization** — Smooth out shaky footage without touching a single slider
- **Natural Language Editing** — Simply describe what you want ("remove the first 10 seconds", "cut when I cough") and VoxCut handles the rest

### Accessibility First
- **Browser-Based** — No downloads, no installations, no system requirements
- **Works on Any Device** — From a budget laptop to a smartphone
- **Zero Learning Curve** — Intuitive interface designed for first-time editors
- **Completely Free** — No subscriptions, no hidden fees, no premium tiers
