## How It Works

### Step 1: Upload Your Raw Video
Simply drag and drop your unedited footage. That's the only manual action required.

### Step 2: Automatic AI Processing
VoxCut's intelligent engine takes over:
- Removes awkward silences and dead air
- Cuts out filler words, coughs, and mistakes
- Identifies the best takes from multiple recordings
- Stabilizes shaky footage automatically

### Step 3: Choose Your Output Format
Tell VoxCut what you're creating:
- **Instagram Reel** — Optimized for vertical, short-form engagement
- **YouTube Video** — Perfect aspect ratio and quality for the platform
- **Cinematic** — Professional color grading and widescreen format
- **TikTok** — Quick, punchy edits for maximum impact
- **Custom** — You decide the specifications

### Step 4: Voice Editing Mode
This is where the magic happens.

After initial processing, VoxCut takes you to the **Voice Editing Screen** — a completely touch-free editing experience:

- *"Trim the first 5 seconds"* — Done.
- *"Add a fade out at the end"* — Done.
- *"Make it brighter"* — Done.
- *"Remove that part where I sneezed"* — Done.
- *"Export to my phone"* — Done.

**No mouse. No keyboard. No touching the screen. Just your voice and your vision.**

### Step 5: Voice-Controlled Export
When you're happy with your edit, just say:
- "Export as Instagram Reel"
- "Save in 4K"
- "Download to my device"

And it's done. Professional content created entirely with your voice.
